FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our running club platform.

How much does the platform cost?

We offer tiered pricing based on your club size and feature requirements. Our starter package begins at £99/month for clubs with up to 100 active members. Enterprise solutions for larger organizations are available with custom pricing. All plans include your own branded app and core features.

How long does implementation take?

Most clubs are up and running within 2 weeks. Our onboarding process includes custom branding setup, data migration from your existing systems, and training for your team. We offer expedited setup for clubs with upcoming events.

Can we integrate with our existing website?

Absolutely! We offer seamless integration with your existing website through our API or embeddable widgets. You can maintain your web presence while adding the functionality of our platform.

How accurate is the live tracking?

Our tracking system uses a combination of GPS, cellular networks, and course checkpoints to provide accuracy within 5-10 meters. Battery optimization ensures minimal impact on participant phones, even for marathon distances.

Can we process payments through the platform?

Yes, our platform includes secure payment processing for event registrations, membership dues, and merchandise sales. We support all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and can set up direct debit for recurring payments.

Do you offer technical support?

All plans include dedicated technical support. Our standard support hours are 8am-8pm on weekdays with email support on weekends. Premium plans include 24/7 support and on-site assistance for major events.
